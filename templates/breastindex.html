About This Tool

This application uses a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) to analyze breast histopathology images and classify them into four categories:

  • Benign: Non-cancerous abnormal cells
  • InSitu: Early-stage cancer cells that haven't spread
  • Invasive: Cancer cells that have spread beyond the original tissue
  • Lymphocytes: Normal immune cells present in the tissues
